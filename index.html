Digital Adoption & Current Experience

54% Have Used QR Code Menus

Top QR Menu Frustrations:

Pitch Angle: Customers are already using QR codes, but they're being let down. MenuCard is built to work flawlessly, even on slower networks, and provides the interactive experience they expect.

What Diners Actually Want

79% Want Speed & Efficiency

Top 3 Most Valued Features:

  • Speed & Efficiency 79%
  • High-Quality Visuals 46%
  • Order Customization 33%
Pitch Angle: The modern diner values their time and wants control. They want to see what they're ordering, customize it to their liking, and get it fast. MenuCard is built around these core principles.
